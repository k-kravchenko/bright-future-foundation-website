Our Mission

Ocean Guardians is a non-profit organization dedicated to reducing plastic pollution in our oceans. We believe that through education, community action, and policy advocacy, we can create lasting change for our planet's marine ecosystems.

Image placeholder

What We Do

Unordered list of activities
  • Organize beach cleanup events in coastal communities
  • Educate schools and communities about plastic pollution
  • Advocate for stronger environmental policies
  • Support research on ocean health and marine life
  • Partner with businesses to reduce single-use plastics
