From Student to Community Leader: Maria's Journey

Maria joined our Adult Literacy Initiative in 2020 with limited English skills and no high school diploma. Through dedicated study and support from our mentors, she not only earned her GED but also completed our Digital Skills Training program.

Today, Maria works as a community outreach coordinator and volunteers as a mentor in the same program that helped her. "Bright Future Foundation didn't just teach me to read and write better," she says. "They showed me that I could achieve anything I set my mind to."

— Maria Rodriguez, Program Graduate & Volunteer Mentor
